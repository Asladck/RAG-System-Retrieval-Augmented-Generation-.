Minecraft is a sandbox game.

It was created by Markus Persson.

He is also known as Notch.

The game was released in 2011.

Minecraft is now owned by Microsoft.

It is one of the best-selling games ever.

The game has no specific goals.

Players can explore infinite worlds.

The world is made of blocks.

Blocks can be broken and placed.

Players can build anything they want.

The main character is Steve.

Another default skin is Alex.

There are different game modes.

Survival mode is very popular.

In Survival mode, you must collect resources.

You can also fight monsters.

Creative mode gives you unlimited blocks.

Adventure mode is for custom maps.

Spectator mode lets you fly through blocks.

The main world is called the Overworld.

There is also the Nether.

And there is a third dimension called The End.

The End is home to the Ender Dragon.

Defeating the Ender Dragon ends the game.

You can respawn after dying.

Players can craft tools.

Tools help you mine faster.

A pickaxe is used for mining stone.

A shovel is used for dirt or sand.

An axe is for cutting wood.

A hoe is used for farming.

There are many ores in the game.

Coal is the most common ore.

Iron is stronger than stone.

Gold is shiny but weak.

Diamonds are very strong.

Emeralds are used for trading.

Redstone is used for machines.

Netherite is stronger than diamond.

You can smelt ores in a furnace.

Food keeps you from starving.

You can cook food too.

Animals drop food when killed.

Chickens drop feathers and meat.

Cows give leather and beef.

Sheep give wool and mutton.

Pigs drop porkchops.

You can grow crops like wheat.

You can make bread with wheat.

Villages can be found in the world.

Villagers live in small houses.

Villagers can trade with you.

Each villager has a job.

A farmer grows crops.

A librarian sells enchanted books.

A blacksmith sells armor.

An armorer also trades metal gear.

A cleric trades potions.

A fletcher trades arrows.

There are different biomes.

A desert biome has sand and cacti.

A forest biome has many trees.

A jungle biome has tall trees.

A plains biome is flat and grassy.

A snowy biome has snow and ice.

A swamp biome has slime.

The ocean biome has coral.

Mountains are tall and rocky.

You can find caves underground.

Caves are full of ores.

Some caves have monsters.

Zombies attack at night.

Skeletons shoot arrows.

Creepers explode when close.

Spiders can climb walls.

Endermen teleport.

Witches throw potions.

Slimes jump and split.

Guardians protect the ocean temples.

The Wither is a dangerous boss.

You can summon the Wither.

You need Wither skeleton skulls.

The Wither drops a Nether Star.

The Nether Star is used for beacons.

Beacons give you powers.

You can build portals.

The Nether portal is made of obsidian.

You light it with flint and steel.

The End portal needs Ender Eyes.

Ender Pearls are dropped by Endermen.

Blaze powder is made from Blaze rods.

Blazes live in Nether fortresses.

Ghasts shoot fireballs.

Piglins trade gold for items.

Hoglins attack players.

You can tame wolves.

You can tame cats.

Horses can be ridden.

You need a saddle to ride a horse
