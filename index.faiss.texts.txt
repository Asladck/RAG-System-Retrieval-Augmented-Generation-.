Suleyman Demirel University (SDU) is located in Kaskelen, Kazakhstan. It was founded in 1996 and named after the former President of Turkey, Suleyman Demirel. SDU offers educational programs in fields such as Engineering, Business, Education, and Humanities. The university has modern laboratories, libraries, and a diverse student community. Its mission is to prepare qualified specialists for the digital economy and promote research and innovation in Central Asia.
